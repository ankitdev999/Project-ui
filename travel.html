Complete travel planning

Our App features all the aspects of travel planning, ranging from flight, bus, or train booking to hotel booking, event booking as well as intercity travel booking. For this reason, our application offers a one-stop solution for all your travel needs.

Intercity Vehicle rentals

We are aware of the awkwardness faced by the people in a new city. We offer a separate cab rental section, with sub features such as self-drive options for various car models. On top of this, we integrate the well-known cab services providers to smoothen your travel.

Virtual destination tour

Our images support VR functionality, enabling travelers to view their choice of destination virtually through their mobile phones. This function allows them to select their tourist location with much ease.

Travel guide

Our app is developed with a separate section of the travel guide that suggests several attractive places for families and friends. Also, we offer images of the destination with the utmost details so that the traveler can decide on their travel preferences.

Upcoming events panel

Along with the travel guide, we have a panel of the forthcoming events nearby your location of the visit so that you can enjoy the live shows or other events that suit your interest.

Filtered and intelligent search

Our Application provides quick and efficient search options for easy recognition along with proficient recently searched items.

Simplified and detailed Admin panel

The admin panel we offer supports attractive UI/UX designing with the users’ past, upcoming, and canceled trips. Through this panel, every piece of information could be tracked by the user.

Covid guidelines

Since the pandemic, people have been hunting for information on covid travel restrictions. We simplify this process by introducing a separate section of covid rules and regulations for all the states. In addition, we make sure we timely update them for the most recent information. We also provide space for the Covid vaccination certificates that are much needed for travel.

Reviews and recommendations

We offer a detailed review section for the users to look at and decide their destination. We also provide separate comments sections for hotel booking and give criterias for cleanliness, location, etc.
